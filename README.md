# 📊 AI-Powered Business Intelligence Dashboard

An end-to-end Business Intelligence solution built using **Power BI** to analyze retail sales data and generate actionable business insights through interactive dashboards, advanced DAX calculations, and data modeling.

---

# 📌 Project Highlights

- ✅ End-to-End Power BI Project
- ✅ Star Schema Data Model
- ✅ 15+ DAX Measures
- ✅ Time Intelligence (YTD, MTD, QTD, YoY)
- ✅ Interactive Multi-Page Dashboard
- ✅ Business Insights & Recommendations

---

# 🎯 Business Objective

This dashboard helps answer key business questions:

- Which region generates the highest sales and profit?
- Which product categories perform the best?
- Which customers generate the highest revenue?
- How do discounts impact profitability?
- What are the sales trends over time?
- Which business areas require improvement?

# 💡 Key Business Insights

- West region generated the highest sales and profit.
- Technology is the best-performing category.
- High discounts reduce profitability.
- South region has comparatively lower profit margins.
- High-value customers contribute significantly to total revenue.

---

# 🚀 Business Recommendations

- Reduce excessive discounts on low-profit products.
- Focus marketing efforts on Technology products.
- Improve pricing strategies in low-profit regions.
- Promote high-margin products.
- Strengthen customer retention through loyalty programs.
